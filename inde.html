DELICIOUS TEA

Tea Stuff

  • water.
  • tea bags
    • Tapal/Everyday.
  • sugar.
  • milk.

Method

  1. Add 1 cup/200 mL of freshly boiled water to your tea bag (in a mug).
  2. Allow the tea bag to brew for 2 minutes.
  3. Remove the tea bag.
  4. Add 10 mL of milk.
  5. Add one spoon sugar.
  6. Shake with spoon.

Your delicious tea is raedy.
